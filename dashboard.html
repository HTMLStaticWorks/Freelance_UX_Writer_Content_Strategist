Style Guide

Maintain consistency across your product with these guidelines.

Voice & Tone

Our voice is helpful, transparent, and direct. We speak to users as a knowledgeable partner.

Punctuation

Use sentence case for headings. No full stops in buttons. Oxford commas are mandatory.

Error Messages

Don't blame the user. Offer a solution. Use active voice and avoid jargon like "Error 404".

Date & Time

Use 24-hour format for system logs and 12-hour for user activity. Relative dates for < 24h.
